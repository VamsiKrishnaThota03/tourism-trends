Data Format Requirements

Your CSV file should contain the following columns:

  • Year - The year of the tourism data
  • Country - The destination country
  • Region - The geographical region
  • Visitor_Count - Number of visitors
  • Visitor_Type - Type of visitor (Business/Leisure)
  • Season - The season of the visit
  • Age_Group - Age group of visitors

Example row:

2023,France,Europe,1500000,Business,Spring,18-24
